From Spreadsheets to Code

I'm a developer.

It just took me ten years to realize that. It's funny how the signs were there when I look back at my previous career as a pricing analyst. When I got tired of doing redundant tasks, I wrote macros in Excel. When I needed data, I wrote SAS and SQL queries to get them myself. I thoroughly enjoyed projects where I worked with consultants to deploy a new system.

So, I made the difficult decision to leave my job and pursue my dream at Dev Bootcamp. Guess what? Best decision I ever made (after marrying my wonderful wife of course).
